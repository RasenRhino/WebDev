Our Mission

  • To locate, access and feed those who truly need help
  • To ensure quantity and quality of fresh, wholesome and nutritious food
  • To locate beneficiaries within a 5-km radius of dispatch centre for timely delivery
  • To provide food trucks to stock the food collected from donors like corporates, hotels and restaurants before distribution
  • To maintain records of donations and distribution of both food and funds
  • To ascertain need-based distribution on a first come first serve basis
