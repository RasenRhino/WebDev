SPREADING AWARENESS

A drawing competition was organized to promote Sabki Thaali and generate awareness for this much needed app. What followed was a glorious morning where young children put their ideas on paper in technicolor. Like the wings of an eagle this idea soared across imagination and transformed into reality. It is hoped that these young children will want to join the brigade of changemakers and in turn transform society. "CATCH THEM YOUNG!!"

FOR A CAUSE

When the cause is just, things fall into place smoothly. On a crisp and sunny day, the Sabki Thaali Community ran. They ran not for personal good health but for the good health of the entire community. It seemed every step taken was one more in the battle to eradicate poverty, to fight malnutrition, to feed a hungry stomach. Leaving behind personal identities and individual choices, each believer wore Sabki Thaali t shirts that proclaimed their faith in the cause. The motto? "We rise by lifting others."

BRIDGING GAPS

The first step towards change is awareness and the second is acceptance. The Sabki Thaali Team approached various corporate houses and hotels and were welcomed with open hearts and wallets. The seminars and workshops brought in bouts of introspection whether earning money is all one wants from life or one also wants to help others in simple ways and experience the satisfaction of participating in a Win-Win situation.
