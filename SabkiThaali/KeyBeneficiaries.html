Key Beneficiaries


Supporting Cottage Industries

This movement will provide job opportunities to MSMEs like small and green cottage industries that sell their pattal leaf plates to ‘Sabki Thaali’ at bulk rates.

Unifying Society

“Sabki Thaali” has taken up the responsibilty of providing a much needed service for both producers and consumers. While offering food to the underprivileged sections we take only a token amount of ₹5 from them to maintain their self esteem.

Corporate Social Responsibilty

The business houses can benefit by providing support to charitable causes, building up CSR initiatives and showing the way for others to emulate.

Youth as Changemakers

This path breaking and innovative approach is a beacon for the conscientious youth, who has the will and the drive to bring about a change, by joining this movement in various capacities from van drivers, to facilitators, distributers and even motivators. All those who wanted to help society but didn’t know how, will find their answers in this movement.
