The Changemakers

At a tender age of life we are taught in schools, “The sharing of food is the basis of social life”. Inspired by this adage, Harsh Agarwal, a bright , young and energetic individual is determined to save the community from starvation. He started on this journey by collecting leftover food from his neighbourhood and giving it to the needy. This was the dawn of “Fight against Hunger” a.k.a. “Sabki Thaali”.

Ridham Bhagat, Co-founder of “Sabki Thaali” is driven with an extraordinary passion for what he believes in. He is spontaneous, lives fully in the moment, and wants to bridge very lacuna with technology based solutions.

Their mission is to sensitize people to their rights and duties vis-a-vis society. Through their dream and passion, they have inspired many likeminded individuals and made a difference to the life and nutritional status of countless others.
